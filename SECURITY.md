### Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in RelayCraft, please **DO NOT** open a public issue. Instead, please report it via email to:

ðŸ“§ **security@relaycraft.dev**

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and provide an estimated timeline for the fix.

### Data Privacy Statement

RelayCraft is designed with a **Privacy-First** architecture:
1.  **Local Processing**: All traffic interception, decryption, and analysis happen **locally on your device**.
2.  **No Uploads**: RelayCraft **NEVER** uploads your captured traffic, headers, or body content to any cloud server.
3.  **AI Analysis**: When you use AI features, only the **specific request metadata** you explicitly select is sent to the LLM provider (e.g., OpenAI/Anthropic) for analysis. You can opt-out of AI features entirely in Settings.
